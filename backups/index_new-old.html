Meet the Candidate

Meet Dr. Sylvain Doré

Sylvain Doré is a renowned brain doctor and former professor of Anesthesiology and Neuroscience at the University of Florida. Originally from Neuville, Québec, he served for thirteen years at UF, rising to the university's Board of Trustees before retiring to focus on his passions for politics, real estate, and philanthropy.

As an active Democrat, Sylvain believes that with the right changes and leadership, Florida can become the safest and most affordable state in the nation. He is running on a platform of affordable housing, lower insurance costs, healthcare access, voting rights, and strong public education — real solutions for working families in Gainesville, Alachua County, Ocala, and Marion County.

Why Dr. Doré

From the Lab to the Legislature

After dedicating decades to academia as a brain doctor, Sylvain felt compelled to extend his reach by engaging in public service. While his work in education and research significantly benefited his students, he sought a broader platform to effect change on a larger scale.

Becoming a state representative will enable Dr. Doré to influence laws and policies that directly affect the lives of countless Floridians — making the state more affordable, accessible, and safe for working families.

Having nurtured success among his students and colleagues, Dr. Doré sees his candidacy as a natural progression toward promoting the well-being of all Floridians through effective governance and leadership. Above all, his deep-rooted desire to care for people drives him to serve.

The Story Behind the Logo

A Brain, a Bridge, and a Name that Means Gold

As a trained brain doctor, Sylvain incorporated the brain into his logo as a symbol of intellect and innovation. With a nod to artificial intelligence, it represents the core of his life's work.

Both the left and right sides of the brain show a connection — symbolizing the importance of bridging different perspectives, whether on the political spectrum or the educational spectrum, linking STEM with the Arts.

The color gold? A simple translation of his last name: Doré — which means "golden" in French.
